Want to display the RSS feed with ticker or scroller,
This plug-in will display RSS feed with simple scroller or ticker. It gradually reveals each item into view from left to right. This will display 1 by 1 with horizontal scroller.

**To display front end**

1. Go to widget menu and drag and drop the "rss scroller" widget to your sidebar location. or 
2. Copy and past the below mentioned code to your desired template location. 

<code><?php if (function_exists (rss_scr_show)) rss_scr_show(); ?></code>

**To Update the Scroller or Ticker setting:**

1. Go to 'rss scroller' link under SETTINGS TAB. 		

== Installation ==

**Installation Instruction & Configuration**  

*   Unpack the *.zip file and extract the /rss-scroller/ folder.    
*   Drop the 'rss-scroller' folder into your 'wp-content/plugins' folder    
*   In word press administration panels, click on plug-in from the menu.    
*   You should see your new 'rss scroller' plug-in listed under Inactive plug-in tab.    
*   To turn the word presses plug-in on, click activate.    
*   Go to 'rss scroller' link under SETTINGS TAB to Add/edit/delete the news.     
*   Copy and paste the mentioned code to your desired template location!.    

<code><?php if (function_exists (rss_scr_show)) rss_scr_show(); ?></code>
